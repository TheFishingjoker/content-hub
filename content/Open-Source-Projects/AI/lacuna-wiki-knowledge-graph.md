    15|Single MCP tool (`wiki`) that gives agents a searchable, compounding personal research graph. Feed it YouTube, arXiv, PDFs, or any URL — runs structured extraction, knowledge accumulates across sessions. Vault is plain markdown, works natively with Obsidian. Hybrid semantic + keyword search via DuckDB index. Has sweep/audit and synthesis clustering features. Python, MIT license, 10 stars. On PyPI as `lacuna-wiki`.
