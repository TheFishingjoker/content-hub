A distraction-free and highly customizable theme for Obsidian by kepano. 4.9k stars, actively maintained with 1,492+ commits. The theme focuses on clean typography, minimal chrome, and extensive color/font customization. Companion to the Minimal Theme Settings plugin. Supports full-width media rendering as of Obsidian 1.12.
