**Seen at — Instagram**: [@github.awesome](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DTmFE6_E830/)

A complete replacement for your crontab that also doubles as a server monitor and ticketing system. Unlike running scripts blindly, XyOps tracks the CPU and RAM of every job. If something crashes, it auto-creates a ticket with a full snapshot of exactly what the server was doing at that moment — no more guessing why your 3 AM cron job failed.
