**Link:** [agent-next/polymarket-paper-trader](https://github.com/agent-next/polymarket-paper-trader)

Paper trading simulator for Polymarket, built for AI agents. Simulates real exchange mechanics: level-by-level orderbook execution, exact Polymarket fee model, slippage tracking, limit orders (GTC/GTD), multi-outcome markets. Exposes 26 MCP tools so AI agents can trade autonomously. CLI (`pm-trader`) with init, browse, buy/sell, portfolio, backtest, leaderboard. 3 example strategies (momentum, mean reversion, limit grid). Multi-account support for A/B testing. Python, SQLite, PyPI. 252 stars, MIT.

This is directly competitive with our polymarket-lab — but MCP-native and more mature on the simulation side. Worth comparing architectures.
