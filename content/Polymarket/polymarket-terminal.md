**Link:** [direkturcrypto/polymarket-terminal](https://github.com/direkturcrypto/polymarket-terminal)

Open-source automated trading terminal for Polymarket. Three strategies: (1) Maker Rebate MM — market-making on 15-min crypto Up-or-Down markets, places simultaneous YES/NO maker limits at combined cost < $1, merges via CTF for spread profit. (2) Copy Trader — mirrors target wallet trades in real-time with proportional sizing. (3) Orderbook Sniper — 3-tier deep-discount GTC limits to catch panic dumps. Node.js 18+, CLOB API, Polygon RPC, WebSocket for fill detection, PM2 on VPS. 230 stars, MIT.

Relevant to our Polymarket research — the market-making strategy is interesting. Could inform backtesting.
